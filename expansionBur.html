The expansion with Burgundy is quite clear. Annex your junior partners and expand in the HRE using your Friendly relations with Austria and expand in to the modern day Low Countries. Then once you have done all that just right click on France and invade them with insufferable amount of mercenary/Own soldier armies and that way crush France within a month or so. Note that such a thing can only happen if you grow to around 600 development.
