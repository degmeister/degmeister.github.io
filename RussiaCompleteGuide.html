Guide to Russia

Russia, is a country that can be formed playing the russian minors or the greater principalility of Moscovy. The merchant republic of Novgorod could also form russia

I would recommended because of its size and power in the early game, take the conquer Novgorod mission and annex as much territory you can while also expanding on the expense of your small nomad tribes. Also watchout for Poland-Lithuania in the start of the game because the strong start Poland get.

Once you have acquired the areas to form Russia, the year should be around 1480 or something like that, your admin tech needs to be at level 10 to form Russia and because of this you should look to expand east as much as you can because of the free development that you can get.

take the expansion idea and the quantity idea because you want to have a vast army with a great amount of land to retreat behind

Once you have formed Russia you can easily attack the Commonwealth and crush them, also again annex as much land as possible because they aren't strong if annex their most industrious regions like Smolensk and Vitebrsk.

also annex the crimean lands as much as possible aswell because it will give you acess to some ports which will give you trade opportunities.
