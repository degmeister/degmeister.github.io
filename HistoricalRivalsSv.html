Swedens Historical Rivals are Russia,Denmark and Poland if you decide to break alliance with them at some point you will have to prepare with atleast one ally to go to war with you, it will help tremedously and it will make the wat alot easier. Sweden can handle both Denmark and Norway easily even post independence war in the 1450:s but Russia is a nation that Sweden cannot (until you has enough development) challenge at all. Allying Poland for beating Russia back is good and it will prevent them from becoming a super power. Poland on the other hand cannot challenge in the 1600-1700, this is the period where Sweden becomes the definition of unstoppable and unchallenged in northern Europe and possibly even compared to France and Great Britain. Use rival wars to gain alot of monarch points long term and you will dominate the game by the late 1700 hundreds and you can easily earn the ''Lion of the north'' achievement. By gaining these cheap and easily earned monarch points you can develop Swedens provinces to become extremly industrious and by the end of the game it's not wierd to have atleast 230 development by your core Swedish territory.
