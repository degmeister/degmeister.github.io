Expansion for Sweden is very hard in the beginning stages and very wise but cautious moves are needed, Sweden is under a union with Denmark and Denmark at the start of the game is very very strong but quickly falls off because of their terrible ruler. Sweden should wait until atleast the year 1450 or 1460 when her manpower is strong enough to combat both norway and Denmark at the same time with Engelbrekt event which if you chose it will give you 10 000 manpower extra on top of what you already have. Once you have succeded in bringing enough allies to defeat Denmark in an independence war you will from the get go have easy prey like the Livonian Order to annex the provinces of Reval, Narwa which are the most developed provinces they have except for Liefland. Other expansion routes are Norway and at last Danmark which will give you enough power in the mid-game to dominate mainland Europe
