France starts the game with two quite large armies and two good generals(two stars). France should not be the one to go on the defensive however that doesn't mean France should just be throwing soldiers at their enemies. France is a country that is quite flat and also has many rivers running across its northern border and to teh south has mountains with only two key invasions points for Spain. Bottlenecking these two points against your enemies will make them waste alot of soldiers in a short amount of time. As such you should always when battling the HRE and Spain, let them come to you and then reinforce with another army close by will shatter their armies and you can easily and most of the time unopposed occupy their territory and annex alot of land while having alot of soldiers left to deal with backstabbing shitbags like Austria and Great Britain.
